Detailed Description

Stores the geometry of a cross-section (profile) extracted from the scene.

The OGR geometry pointer holds the 2-D polygon or line-string outline of the section. cline references the spine/centre-line entity from which this section was generated. changed signals the renderer that the data must be re-uploaded.
