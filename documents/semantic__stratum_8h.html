enum SpineType

Spine geometry type for geological surface extrusion.

Selects the curve shape of the extrusion spine used when building a 3-D geological interface surface from a 2-D boundary wire.

  • Linear straight-line spine (default).
  • Arc circular arc via GC_MakeArcOfCircle(P_top, V_top_tangent, P_bot).
  • Parabola quadratic Bezier/BSpline whose control point is the intersection of the tangent lines at the top and bottom endpoints.
