Detailed Description

Wraps an OpenCASCADE topological shape (TopoDS_Shape) as an ECS component.

Tracks additional flags indicating whether the shape is a 2-D profile, a closed polygon, and its general topology kind (mapped to TopAbs_ShapeEnum).
